1.  **Product Titles:** Ensure Amazon product titles on our site include the *Category* keyword (e.g., instead of just "C1000", use "Anker Solix C1000 *Solar Generator*").

    *   "Top 5 Smokeless Fire Pits for 2026"

4.  **Image Alt Text:** Always describe the image using keywords (e.g., "Modern wicker patio furniture set on a wooden deck with solar lights").
